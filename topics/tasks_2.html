Tasks

A task is a specific result that may be reached by completing some steps or actions. From the technical point of view we consider for task a specific container with combination of steps that are needed to be undertaken in order to achieve a specific state or result.

Procedure (workflow) - is a specific type of Task that is a more formal, structured and strict set of steps that must be followed in a specific order, often with specific guidelines or regulations in place. Each task is assigned to a specific Role and may be assigned to more than one Role. The Role needs special permission (glossary term) to handle this specific task. Each Role handles its tasks using specific workspace.
